Operation Dandelion
The book The Main in the High Castle by Philip K. Dick, supposes an alternate history where the AXis won in WW2. Set in 1962, The German government plans a surprise nuclear attack on Japan, before the Japanese complete the development of their own nuclear weapons.
Germans win by taking Manchuria (from where they can nuke the Japanese easily. The Japanese win by surviving 12 turns (at which point they have their own nukes.
The initital German strike is reflected by Japan having 0 starting resources
By Roger Cooper & Major Arcana
